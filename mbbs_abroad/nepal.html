Licensing & Career Pathways After MBBS

Your roadmap to medical practice in India and globally after completing MBBS in Nepal.

FMGE / NExT (India)

Practice in India

Clear the NMC's Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) or NExT to register and practice as a doctor in India. NMC Approved: Yes

USMLE / PLAB (Global)

Global Practice

Pursue USMLE for USA residency, PLAB for UK practice, or AMC for Australia. A Nepal MBBS degree gives you global flexibility.

PG Specialization

MS / MD / DNB

Appear for NEET-PG to pursue MD/MS specializations in India. Many graduates also pursue PG programs in Europe or Canada post licensing.

Frequently Asked Questions - MBBS in Nepal

Get answers to common questions about studying medicine in Nepal.

Nepal offers affordable MBBS education with good FMGE pass rates and regional familiarity. Kathmandu Medical College provides quality education at competitive prices with English-medium instruction and cultural similarity to India.
FMGE pass rates for Nepalese universities range from 25-30%. Kathmandu Medical College has the highest pass rate around 30%, followed by B.P. Koirala Institute at 28%. The pass rates are competitive with good preparation.
Yes, Nepal is safe for international students. Kathmandu and other major cities have good infrastructure and public transport. Universities provide secure campus environments and there is strong cultural similarity with India.
Monthly living expenses in Nepal range from ₹19,000-29,000 including food, transportation, and miscellaneous expenses. Hostel fees are ₹1.0-1.2 lakhs per year. The cost of living is reasonable and affordable for international students.
Yes, you can practice medicine in India after completing MBBS from Nepal. All recommended universities are NMC-approved, so you are eligible to appear for FMGE/NExT. After clearing the screening test, you can register with State Medical Council and practice in India.
MBBS in Nepal is a 5.5-year program including 1 year of internship. The curriculum follows international standards with emphasis on practical training. First 4.5 years focus on basic sciences and clinical subjects, final year is internship. Teaching is in English with modern facilities.
