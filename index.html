Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and studying abroad.

The visa processing time varies by country. Generally, it takes 2-8 weeks for most destinations. USA F1 visa processing can take 3-5 weeks, UK Tier 4 visa typically takes 3 weeks, Canada study permit takes 4-8 weeks, and Australia student visa takes 4-6 weeks. We recommend starting the process at least 3-4 months before your intended intake.
Key documents include: 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates, NEET scorecard (mandatory for Indian students), valid passport, passport-size photographs, medical fitness certificate, HIV/AIDS test report, bank statements showing financial capability, and admission letter from the university. Additional documents may be required based on the specific country and university requirements.
Yes! We offer comprehensive coaching for IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, GRE, GMAT, and SAT. Our programs include expert faculty, extensive practice materials, regular mock tests, and personalized feedback. We offer both online and offline classes with flexible batch timings to suit working professionals and students.
MBBS costs vary significantly by country. Georgia and Kyrgyzstan offer programs starting from ₹3-5 lakhs per year. Russia and Kazakhstan range from ₹3-6 lakhs annually. Philippines and China (when available) typically cost ₹4-8 lakhs per year. Total costs include tuition, accommodation, food, and other living expenses. We provide detailed cost breakdowns for each destination.
Yes, NEET qualification is mandatory for Indian students pursuing MBBS abroad as per the National Medical Commission (NMC) guidelines. Students must qualify NEET to be eligible for admission to foreign medical universities. The NEET score is valid for 3 years for MBBS abroad admissions. This requirement ensures students can later practice in India after clearing FMGE/NExT.
