The choice depends on your target country and institution. IELTS is widely accepted in UK, Australia, Canada, and New Zealand. TOEFL is preferred by US universities. Preparation time varies by test and your current proficiency level. For English tests (IELTS/TOEFL/PTE): 1-3 months for intermediate learners, 2-4 weeks for advanced learners. PTE: 58-73 is commonly accepted. GRE: 310-320 for decent programs, 325+ for top universities. GMAT: 650-700 for good business schools, 720+ for top programs. We help you identify specific requirements for your target universities.
