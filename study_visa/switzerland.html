Student Visa & Living Information

Essential details for studying and living in Switzerland

Student Visa Requirements

  • ✓ Admission letter from Swiss university
  • ✓ Proof of financial means (CHF 2,400/month)
  • ✓ Valid passport (minimum 6 months)
  • ✓ Health insurance coverage
  • ✓ Proof of accommodation
  • ✓ Background certificate from home country

Living Cost & Scholarships

  • Monthly Living Expenses: CHF 2,400-3,500
  • Accommodation: CHF 800-1,500/month
  • Food & Transport: CHF 600-900/month
  • Scholarships Available: 20-30% of students
  • Work Permits: 15 hours/week during studies
  • Post-Study Work: 6-12 months permit available
