Licensing & Career Pathways After MBBS

Your roadmap to medical practice in India and globally after completing MBBS in Egypt.

FMGE / NExT (India)

Practice in India

Clear the NMC's Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) or NExT to register and practice as a doctor in India. NMC Approved: Yes

USMLE / PLAB (Global)

Global Practice

Pursue USMLE for USA residency, PLAB for UK practice, or AMC for Australia. A Egypt MBBS degree gives you global flexibility.

PG Specialization

MS / MD / DNB

Appear for NEET-PG to pursue MD/MS specializations in India. Many graduates also pursue PG programs in Europe or Canada post licensing.

Frequently Asked Questions - MBBS in Egypt

Get answers to common questions about studying medicine in Egypt.

Egypt offers a unique combination of ancient civilization and modern medical education. Universities like Cairo University provide excellent clinical training, international recognition, and affordable fees. The Middle Eastern location offers great networking opportunities.
FMGE pass rates for Egyptian universities range from 18-22%. Cairo University has the highest pass rate around 22%, followed by Alexandria University at 20%. The pass rates are competitive with good preparation provided by universities.
Yes, Egypt is generally safe for international students. Major cities like Cairo and Alexandria have good infrastructure and security. Universities provide secure campus environments and there is a significant international student community including many Indians.
Monthly living expenses in Egypt range from ₹16,000-25,000 including food, transportation, and miscellaneous expenses. Hostel fees are ₹0.6-0.8 lakhs per year. The cost of living is reasonable, especially in university cities.
Yes, you can practice medicine in India after completing MBBS from Egypt. All recommended universities are NMC-approved, so you are eligible to appear for FMGE/NExT. After clearing the screening test, you can register with State Medical Council and practice in India.
MBBS in Egypt is a 6-year program including 1 year of internship. The curriculum follows international standards with emphasis on practical training. First 5 years focus on basic sciences and clinical subjects, final year is internship. Teaching is in English with modern facilities.
