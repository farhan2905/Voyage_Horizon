Licensing & Career Pathways After MBBS

Your roadmap to medical practice in India and globally after completing MBBS in Uzbekistan.

FMGE / NExT (India)

Practice in India

Clear the NMC's Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) or NExT to register and practice as a doctor in India. NMC Approved: Yes

USMLE / PLAB (Global)

Global Practice

Pursue USMLE for USA residency, PLAB for UK practice, or AMC for Australia. A Uzbekistan MBBS degree gives you global flexibility.

PG Specialization

MS / MD / DNB

Appear for NEET-PG to pursue MD/MS specializations in India. Many graduates also pursue PG programs in Europe or Canada post licensing.

Frequently Asked Questions - MBBS in Uzbekistan

Get answers to common questions about studying medicine in Uzbekistan.

Uzbekistan offers the most affordable MBBS education among NMC-approved countries with excellent FMGE pass rates. Tashkent Medical Academy provides world-class facilities and English-medium education at very competitive prices.
FMGE pass rates for Uzbekistan universities range from 25-30%. Tashkent Medical Academy has the highest pass rate around 28%, followed by Andijan State Medical Institute at 26%. The pass rates are excellent and students receive good preparation.
Yes, Uzbekistan is generally safe for international students. Tashkent is a modern, well-developed city with good infrastructure and public transport. Universities provide secure campus environments and there is a growing Indian student community.
Monthly living expenses in Uzbekistan range from ₹11,000-19,000 including food, transportation, and miscellaneous expenses. Hostel fees are ₹0.5-0.6 lakhs per year. The cost of living is very reasonable, especially in Tashkent.
Yes, you can practice medicine in India after completing MBBS from Uzbekistan. All recommended universities are NMC-approved, so you are eligible to appear for FMGE/NExT. After clearing the screening test, you can register with State Medical Council and practice in India.
MBBS in Uzbekistan is a 6-year program including 1 year of internship. The curriculum follows international standards with emphasis on practical training. First 5 years focus on basic sciences and clinical subjects, final year is internship. Teaching is in English with modern facilities.
