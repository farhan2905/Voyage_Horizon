Licensing & Career Pathways After MBBS

Your roadmap to medical practice in India and globally after completing MBBS in Russia.

FMGE / NExT (India)

Practice in India

Clear the NMC's Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) or NExT to register and practice as a doctor in India. NMC Approved: Yes

USMLE / PLAB (Global)

Global Practice

Pursue USMLE for USA residency, PLAB for UK practice, or AMC for Australia. A Russia MBBS degree gives you global flexibility.

PG Specialization

MS / MD / DNB

Appear for NEET-PG to pursue MD/MS specializations in India. Many graduates also pursue PG programs in Europe or Canada post licensing.

Frequently Asked Questions - MBBS in Russia

Yes, many Russian universities offer MBBS programs in English medium for international students. However, some universities may offer bilingual programs (English + Russian). We recommend universities with complete English-medium instruction that are NMC-approved.
Monthly living expenses in Russia range from ₹20,000-31,000 including food, transportation, and miscellaneous expenses. Hostel fees are ₹1-1.8 lakhs per year. Food costs are higher than in some other destinations, but the quality of education justifies the investment.
